About Me

Hello there! First of all, thank you for the time you took to check out my portfolio and see the projects I’ve been working on so far. I hope you will enjoy them! Also, I would like to introduce myself so you can get to know me a little better.

My name is Goran Ljiljak and I’m a Geek. I’ve been fascinated by technology since my childhood, so I can say I’m a huge tech enthusiast.

I studied mechanical engineering at FTN and during my college years I got a job as a Data Analyst in an e-commerce company. I worked in this industry for 6 years. Although I enjoyed my job, I realised something was missing. Through time I decided to pursue my childhood dream - programming - and it was the best decision I could have ever made. I finished the .NET development course via FTN Informatika, and during my first classes I started implementing new skills and knowledge into my own work.

Although I realise I am only beginning to walk in developer’s shoes, I am extremely eager to master new technologies and I want to be surrounded with people who share the same passion towards technology - and to learn from the more experienced ones on a daily basis.

I am driven to succeed with the love and passion I have for this industry, and through hard work and constant learning I believe I will be a great asset to your company as a beginner, and in years to come.

I am looking forward to talking with you and sharing my thoughts on the importance of technology to the world and to me as an individual as well.
